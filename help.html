Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get a verification badge?

You can request verification from your profile. Once approved, the verified badge will be visible publicly on your profile.

Why is my verification request pending?

Verification requests are reviewed manually. This may take some time. Please wait for approval.

Didn't receive the password reset email?

Check your Spam folder. If you still don't see the email, try again.

How do I delete my account?

Account deletion options are available in your account settings. If you face issues, contact support below.

Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to MyLook. Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the MyLook application.

2. Information We Collect

  • Basic profile information such as name, username, and bio
  • Profile photo and public account details
  • App usage data to improve user experience

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage user profiles
  • To display public profile information to other users
  • To improve app features, security, and performance

4. Verification Badge

MyLook provides a verification badge for approved accounts. If your account is verified, the badge will be publicly visible on your profile so other users can easily identify verified accounts.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Public profile data (such as username and verification status) may be visible to all users of the app.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us through the MyLook app.
